Find your future today.

The Majors Marketplace is your first step to finding your passion in the College of Liberal Arts & Sciences at KU.

Using tools and information in the Majors Marketplace, you can confidently choose a direction for your college career and beyond. You can search for a specific major or browse through more than 40 undergraduate majors offered in the College. Explore degrees in a wide variety of subjects from global studies, arts and humanities to social sciences, math and natural sciences.

You have questions and we have answers. Whether you’re curious to learn more about what it takes to complete a major, opportunities beyond the classroom, or what careers may await you after graduation, you’ve come to the right place.
