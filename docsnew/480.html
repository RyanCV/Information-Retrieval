Counseling Psychology

The department of Educational Psychology offers both a Master's and Doctorate degree in counseling psychology prepare individuals to work as professional counselors and as counseling psychologists, respectively.


Degrees offered:
  •  M.S.  
  •  Ph.D.  

Educational Psychology and Research

The Department of Educational Psychology offers both a Master's and Doctoral degree in Educational Psychology and Research (EPR). The program also allows students to select from two different program tracks: Development & Learning -OR- Research, Evaluation, Measurement & Statistics.


Degrees offered:
  •  M.S.E  
  •  Ph.D.  

School Psychology

The Department of Educational Psychology offers a School Psychology programs that combines coursework in both theory and research with supervised practice in public school settings to prepare individuals to work in public school, university and community settings.


Degrees offered:
  •  Ed.S.  
  •  Ph.D.
