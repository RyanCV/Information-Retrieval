How to Declare a Major

Major Declaration

A good portion of our majors do not have any requirements in order to declare a major, see list below. All you need to do is contact the department, either stop by or call, and ask about declaring a major.  Some department will want to meet with you before they fill out the Major Declaration Form, while others will fill out the form and encourage you to meet with an advisor at a later time.  Once you have the form filled out you need to bring the top copy to 109 Strong Hall for us to officially declare you in your major.

Major Admission

 

Some of our departments require you to meet certain curriculum criteria before being admitted in the major.  During the semester that you are enrolled in your last course for admission you will need to meet with the department to fill out a Major Intent Form, if you successfully complete your requirements and earn the required GPA in those requirement courses you will be admitted if you have a Major Intent Form on file.

Major Admission Departments
American Studies
Applied Behavioral Sciences
Biology
Communication Studies
Film Media Studies
Global International Studies
Linguistics
Political Science
Psychology
Religious Studies
Sociology

 

Change of Interest

 

You can change your interest at any point in time during your academic career.  We highly encourage you to meet with an advisor in that area before switching.  All you need to do to switch, add or delete your interest in a degree program is to come to 109 Strong Hall and fill out a Major Interest Change Form.  Just remember, this does not officially declare you in that major.
