    $kuj(document).ready(function() {

            var weatherval = '<div class="sectiontitle">Current Weather</div>';
            var date = new Date();
            var minutes = 30;
            date.setTime(date.getTime() + (minutes * 60 * 1000));
            try {
            //cross script issue with IE8 workaround
            if (navigator.appVersion.match(/MSIE/) && window.XDomainRequest) {
                // Use Microsoft XDR
                var xdr = new XDomainRequest();
                xdr.open("get", url);
                xdr.onload = function () {
                    var result = weatherval+xdr.responseText;
                    $kuj.cookie('myweather', result, { expires: date });
                    $kuj('#weatherholder').html(result);
                };
                xdr.send();
            } else if (!navigator.appVersion.match(/MSIE/)) {
                $kuj.ajax({
                    type: 'GET',
                    url: url,
                    success: function (data) {
                        date.setTime(date.getTime() + (minutes * 60 * 1000));
                        $kuj.cookie('myweather', weatherval+data, { expires: date });
                        $kuj('#weatherholder').html($kuj.cookie('myweather'));
                    }
                });
            }
        } catch (e) {}
        }else{
            $kuj('#weatherholder').html($kuj.cookie('myweather'));
        }

    });

        <div class="field field-name-field-topcontent field-type-text-long field-label-hidden"><div class="field-items"><div class="field-item even"><h2>The&nbsp;ILS&nbsp;is the interdisciplinary academic home for leadership studies at KU</h2>

<p>The Institute for Leadership Studies (ILS) supports KUs mission of educating students who can effectively engage in the process of leadership with innovative, interdisciplinary academic programs. Through the ILS these academic programs are collaboratively designed to educate and prepare students to make a difference from where they are with what they have, developing the courage and leadership skills to ignite positive change.&nbsp;</p>

<p><strong>The ILS&nbsp;currently houses:</strong></p>

<ul>
	<li>the <a href="http://ils.drupal.ku.edu/leadership-studies-minor-0">Leadership Studies Minor</a></li>
	<li>the <a href="/kansas-womens-leadership-institute">Kansas Womens Leadership Institute </a></li>
	<li>and the <a href="/overview">Online Certificate for Leadership Strategies and Applications</a></li>
</ul>

<p><strong>Preparations for additional programs are underway and future programming will include:</strong></p>

<ul>
	<li>Study Abroad</li>
	<li>Undergraduate curriculum</li>
	<li>Graduate curriculum</li>
	<li>Graduate certificates</li>
	<li>Alumni programming</li>
	<li>Faculty and staff development</li>
	<li>Research and grant development</li>
	<li>Womens leadership programs and curriculum</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>    </article>

    <div class="homepage-bottomleft">
        <div class="field field-name-field-bottomleft field-type-text-long field-label-hidden"><div class="field-items"><div class="field-item even"><h2>Interdisciplinary Leadership Studies Minor</h2>

<p>First offered to KU students in the spring of 2001, the<a href="http://ils.drupal.ku.edu/leadership-studies-minor-0"> Leadership Studies Minor (LSM)</a> began as a collaborative partnership between the Department of Communication Studies and the Student Involvement and Leadership Center. The first&nbsp;LSM&nbsp;cohort graduated in the spring of 2003. Believing that a complementary interdisciplinary approach allows students to develop a breadth of innovative capacity that can cross disciplinary boundaries, the&nbsp;LSM&nbsp;fosters a tolerance for ambiguity, empathy, perspective taking, and a self-awareness that promotes active reflection and elevates multiple perspectives in diagnosis and problem solving. The&nbsp;ILS&nbsp;continues to design and offer dynamic academic programming in partnership with departments and schools across the university to provide a foundation from which students can engage their disciplinary content with greater efficacy to then foster effective leadership skills within their major content area.</p>

<p><a class="ku_button ku_button-blue" href="http://ils.drupal.ku.edu/leadership-studies-minor-0"><span class="ku_button_content">Learn more</span></a></p>
</div></div></div>    </div>

    <div class="homepage-bottomright">
        <div class="field field-name-field-bottomright field-type-text-long field-label-hidden"><div class="field-items"><div class="field-item even"><h2>Research</h2>

<p>It is also the focus of the&nbsp;ILS&nbsp;to elevate the universitys research contributions in the field of leadership and expand grant development opportunities. By maximizing the universitys intellectual capacity, the&nbsp;ILS&nbsp;generates research and delivers academic programs that foster effective leadership among students, the university community, alumni, and external constituencies. In doing so, the&nbsp;ILS&nbsp;fulfills its mission to create leadership capacity among all of its stakeholders for thriving in the face of challenge, conflict, and change.</p>

<p><a class="ku_button ku_button-blue" href="/research"><span class="ku_button_content">Learn more</span></a></p>
</div></div></div>    </div>
