    //Use javascript to avoid weather caching
    $kuj(document).ready(function() {
        if(!$kuj.cookie('myweather')){
            var url = '//webmedia.ku.edu/~http/ssi/2012/widgets/cmslawrenceweather.php';
            var weatherval = '<div class="sectiontitle">Current Weather</div>';
            var date = new Date();
            var minutes = 30;
            date.setTime(date.getTime() + (minutes * 60 * 1000));
            try {
            //cross script issue with IE8 workaround
            if (navigator.appVersion.match(/MSIE/) && window.XDomainRequest) {
                // Use Microsoft XDR
                var xdr = new XDomainRequest();
                xdr.open("get", url);
                xdr.onload = function () {
                    var result = weatherval+xdr.responseText;
                    $kuj.cookie('myweather', result, { expires: date });
                    $kuj('#weatherholder').html(result);
                };
                xdr.send();
            } else if (!navigator.appVersion.match(/MSIE/)) {
                $kuj.ajax({
                    type: 'GET',
                    url: url,
                    success: function (data) {
                        date.setTime(date.getTime() + (minutes * 60 * 1000));

                    }
                });

        <div class="field field-name-field-topcontent field-type-text-long field-label-hidden"><div class="field-items"><div class="field-item even"><h1>Internal Audit</h1>

<p><strong>Our Mission</strong>: Internal Audit provides independent, objective assurance and advisory services designed to add value and improve the operations of the University of Kansas and its controlled affiliated corporations. We help the University accomplish its objectives by bringing a systematic, disciplined approach to evaluate and improve the effectiveness of risk management, control, and governance processes.</p>

<p><strong>Our Vision:</strong> Internal Audit will provide value-added audits and advisory services with objectivity, fairness, openness, and in accordance with the highest professional and ethical standards. We will build leadership and expertise within our office to help drive positive change and promote integrity in our organization. We will continually improve our operations and strive toward being a leader in the internal audit profession.</p>

<h3>A "One-University" office, providing assurance and advisory services to:</h3>

<ul>
	<li><a href="http://www.ku.edu/"><span style="color: rgb(0, 0, 205);">Lawrence campus</span></a><font size="2"><span style="color: rgb(0, 0, 205);">&nbsp;</span>(and its controlled affiliated corporations)</font></li>
	<li><a href="http://www.kumc.edu/"><span style="color: rgb(0, 0, 205);">Medical Center</span></a><font color="#0066cc"> </font>campuses (and&nbsp;their controlled affiliated corporations)</li>
	<li><a href="http://www.edwardscampus.ku.edu/"><span style="color: rgb(0, 0, 205);">Edwards campus</span></a></li>
</ul>

<p>Internal Audit conducts operational, compliance, and financial-related audits and investigations for the University of Kansas in accordance with the International Professional Practices Framework promulgated by the Institute of&nbsp;Internal Auditors. Our reporting relationships to the Chancellor, the Kansas Board of Regents, and the University's chief business officer help ensure independence and objectivity in the performance of our work. We further assist University departments and other units by providing training and consultation on good business and internal&nbsp;control practices.</p>
