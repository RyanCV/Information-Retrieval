        <div class="field field-name-field-bottomright field-type-text-long field-label-hidden"><div class="field-items"><div class="field-item even"><p class="accordion" style="font-size: 14.3999996185303px;">&nbsp; &nbsp;When to Call 911?​</p>

<div class="accordion_content" style="font-size: 14.3999996185303px;">
<p style="margin: 0px 0px 1em 2em; font-family: inherit; font-style: inherit; font-weight: inherit; padding: 0px; border: 0px; vertical-align: baseline; list-style: decimal; line-height: 1.1em;">POLICE&nbsp;- If you should see someone committing a crime, a person who is about to commit a crime, or a crime has already occurred.</p>

<p style="margin: 0px 0px 1em 2em; font-family: inherit; font-style: inherit; font-weight: inherit; padding: 0px; border: 0px; vertical-align: baseline; list-style: decimal; line-height: 1.1em;">FIRE&nbsp;- On all actual fires, smell of smoke or gas, or if you should see smoke rising from an object that is on fire.</p>

<p style="margin: 0px 0px 1em 2em; font-family: inherit; font-style: inherit; font-weight: inherit; padding: 0px; border: 0px; vertical-align: baseline; list-style: decimal; line-height: 1.1em;">MEDICAL&nbsp;- If a person should need immediate medical attention.</p>
</div>

<p class="accordion" style="font-size: 14.3999996185303px;">&nbsp; &nbsp;What to do when you call 911?​</p>

<div class="accordion_content" style="font-size: 14.3999996185303px;">
<ol>
	<li>Give the&nbsp;location of the emergency.</li>
	<li>State what&nbsp;type of emergency&nbsp;that you have.</li>
	<li>Give the dispatcher&nbsp;your name.</li>
	<li>Give the dispatcher&nbsp;your phone number&nbsp;and what location you are calling from if it differs from the location of the emergency.</li>
</ol>

<p><strong style="color: rgb(68, 68, 68); font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: 13px; line-height: 17.7280006408691px; background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);">Non-Emergency Telephone Numbers</strong><span style="color: rgb(68, 68, 68); font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; line-height: 17.7280006408691px; background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);">&nbsp;If you have a non-emergency situation, call 864-5900 or 864-5913. If you would like to report a crime anonymously, call KU&nbsp;CRIMESTOPPERS&nbsp;at 864-8888. You can anonymously provide information that may lead to the arrest of suspects who have committed a crime.</span></p>
