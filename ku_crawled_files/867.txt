    $kuj(document).ready(function() {
        if(!$kuj.cookie('myweather')){
            var url = '//webmedia.ku.edu/~http/ssi/2012/widgets/cmslawrenceweather.php';
            var weatherval = '<div class="sectiontitle">Current Weather</div>';
            var date = new Date();
            var minutes = 30;
            date.setTime(date.getTime() + (minutes * 60 * 1000));
            try {
            //cross script issue with IE8 workaround
            if (navigator.appVersion.match(/MSIE/) && window.XDomainRequest) {
                // Use Microsoft XDR
                var xdr = new XDomainRequest();
                xdr.open("get", url);
                xdr.onload = function () {
                    var result = weatherval+xdr.responseText;
                    $kuj.cookie('myweather', result, { expires: date });
                    $kuj('#weatherholder').html(result);
                };
                xdr.send();
            } else if (!navigator.appVersion.match(/MSIE/)) {
                $kuj.ajax({
                    type: 'GET',
                    url: url,
                    success: function (data) {
                        date.setTime(date.getTime() + (minutes * 60 * 1000));
                        $kuj.cookie('myweather', weatherval+data, { expires: date });
                        $kuj('#weatherholder').html($kuj.cookie('myweather'));
                    }
                });
            }
        } catch (e) {}
        }else{
            $kuj('#weatherholder').html($kuj.cookie('myweather'));
        }

    });

                                <h1 class="title" id="page-title">Graduate programs</h1>
                    <span property="dc:title" content="Graduate programs" class="rdf-meta element-hidden"></span>    
    <div class="content clearfix">
        <div class="field field-name-body field-type-text-with-summary field-label-hidden"><div class="field-items"><div class="field-item even" property="content:encoded"><dl><dd> </dd>
	<dt><strong>Edwards Campus</strong></dt>
	<dt><a href="http://edwardscampus.ku.edu/overview-masters-applied-statistics-analytics" target="_blank">Applied Statistics &amp; Analytics (MS)</a></dt>
	<dd>Data analysts are in high demand. This graduate program offers students practical computing and problem-solving skills that can be applied in careers involving healthcare, transportation, insurance and manufacturing among others.  Designed for working professionals, this program is flexible and can be completed 100% online.</dd>
	<dt> </dt>
	<dt><strong>School of Business</strong></dt>
	<dt><a href="http://onlinemba.ku.edu/online-programs/online-mba/" target="_blank">Business Administration (MBA)</a></dt>
	<dd>The online MBA program offers three focus areas of study, finance, marketing, and management and leadership, which are all designed to help working professionals advance in their careers.</dd>
	<dt> </dt>
	<dt><strong>School of Education</strong></dt>
	<dt><a href="http://educationonline.ku.edu/online-programs/curriculum-and-instruction/" target="_blank">Curriculum and Instruction (MSE)</a></dt>
	<dd>Designed for current educators, students in this online program focus on applying current theory, research, and technology to curriculum design. Students will learn to use assessment, classroom management and other techniques to strengthen instruction.</dd>
	<dt><a href="http://educationonline.ku.edu/online-programs/reading/" target="_blank">Curriculum and Instruction - Reading (MSE)</a></dt>
	<dd>This online program leverages curriculum and instructional strategies to prepare educators as literary specialists.</dd>
	<dt><a href="https://educationonline.ku.edu/online-programs/tesol/" target="_blank">Curriculum and Instruction - TESOL (MSE)</a></dt>
	<dd>Teaching English to Speakers of Other Languages (TESOL) is a practice that can be incorporated into any classroom, regardless of your instructional background. This online program is available as a full master of science degree for those looking to apply their skills either inside or outside of the classroom setting.</dd>
	<dt><a href="http://elps.soe.ku.edu/academics/edadmin/mse" target="_blank">Educational Administration (MSE)</a></dt>
	<dd>The master of science degree in education administration shapes experienced pre-K-12 educators to be effective leaders by focusing on educational policy, pedagogy, and practical experience in the field. This program requires face-to-face meetings as well as online study.  A separate <a href="http://educationonline.ku.edu/online-programs/educational-administration/">fully online program</a> (with internship) is also available.</dd>
	<dt><a href="http://educationonline.ku.edu/online-programs/masters-secondary-special-education-transitions/" target="_blank">Secondary Special Ed and Transition (MSE)</a></dt>
	<dd>The online master of science degree in transition education and services provides secondary educators with the skills needed to help students with disabilities transition to adult life.</dd>
	<dt><a href="http://educationonline.ku.edu/masters-in-special-ed" target="_blank">Special Education (MSE and Endorsement)</a></dt>
	<dd>Led by faculty who are demonstrated leaders and innovators within the field of special education, this online program prepares you to compassionately and expertly engage with students who have mild-to-moderate special needs.</dd>
	<dt> </dt>
	<dt><strong>School of Health Professions</strong></dt>
	<dt><a href="http://dietetics.kumc.edu/master-of-science-distance.html" target="_blank">Dietetics and Nutrition (MS)</a></dt>
	<dd>The master’s degree in dietetics and nutrition is offered through a partnership with the Great Plains Interactive Distance Education Alliance. The online program provides professionals in nutrition and other careers with a science background the opportunity to advance their knowledge in the science of nutrition.</dd>
	<dt><a href="http://www.kumc.edu/school-of-health-professions/occupational-therapy-education/doctor-of-occupational-therapy.html" target="_blank">Occupational Therapy (OTD)</a></dt>
	<dd>Designed for therapists working in the profession, the online doctor of occupational therapy program offers advanced study in evidence-based practice, professional leadership, specialty practice and teaching.</dd>
	<dt> </dt>
	<dt><strong>School of Journalism and Mass Communications</strong></dt>
	<dt><a href="http://journalism.ku.edu/online-program-digital-content-strategy" target="_blank">Digital Content Strategy (MSJ)</a></dt>
	<dd>Students in this online program will learn how to find and analyze data to solve organizational problems. The program consists of two graduate certificates that can be completed individually or taken together to earn the master's degree. </dd>
	<dt> </dt>
	<dt><strong>School of Nursing</strong></dt>
	<dt><a href="http://nursing.kumc.edu/academics/doctor-of-nursing-practice.html" target="_blank">Nursing (DNP)</a></dt>
	<dd>The doctor of nursing practice degree offers students the opportunity to explore evidence-based practice and learn how to be leaders in the health care community. The program is available for students with either a BS or MS in nursing.  Face-to-face meetings as well as online study are required.</dd>
	<dt><a href="http://nursing.kumc.edu/academics/master-of-science.html" target="_blank">Nursing (MS)</a></dt>
	<dd>The online master of science degree in nursing provides advanced study in essential nursing skills, leadership, and research.  Four specialty areas are currently available: clinical research management, nursing informatics, organizational leadership, and public health nursing.</dd>
	<dt><a href="http://nursing.kumc.edu/academics/doctor-of-philosophy.html" target="_blank">Nursing (PhD)</a></dt>
	<dd>The only doctorate of nursing available in the region, this program prepares graduates for faculty positions, research and philosophical endeavors, and leadership roles in nursing administration. This program requires face-to-face meetings as well as online study.</dd>
	<dt> </dt>
	<dt><strong>School of Pharmacy</strong></dt>
	<dt><a href="http://www.pharmchem.ku.edu/~phch-web/cgi-bin/mydrupal/?q=node/21" target="_blank">Pharmaceutical Chemistry (MS)</a></dt>
	<dd>The online master of science degree in pharmaceutical chemistry enables those working in the private sector or at collaborating institutions to pursue advanced study in the physical/chemical sciences as they relate to pharmaceutical research.</dd>
	<dt> </dt>
	<dt><strong>School of Social Welfare</strong></dt>
	<dt><a href="http://www.socwel.ku.edu/academics/degrees/msw/regular/curriculum" target="_blank">Social Work (MSW)</a></dt>
	<dd>The master of social work degree prepares students for advanced practice in the clinical social work field or social work administration. This program requires face-to-face meetings as well as online study.</dd>
	<dd> </dd>
</dl><h1>Graduate certificates</h1>

<dl><dt><strong>School of Business</strong></dt>
	<dt><a href="http://onlinemba.ku.edu/online-programs/mba-certificate/" target="_blank">Foundations of Business</a></dt>
	<dd>Learn the basics of business theory and practice and apply them in your career or education plans. Credits earned from this online certificate may be applied to the full <a href="http://onlinemba.ku.edu/online-programs/online-mba/" target="_blank">online MBA program</a>.</dd>
</dl><dl><dt><strong>School of Education</strong></dt>
	<dt><a href="http://educationonline.ku.edu/online-programs/autism-spectrum-disorder-graduate-certificate/" target="_blank">Autism Spectrum Disorder</a></dt>
	<dd>This online certificate program prepares educators, administrators, parents and advocates to work with children and students across the autism spectrum.</dd>
	<dt><a href="https://educationonline.ku.edu/online-programs/leadership-in-special-and-inclusive-education-graduate-certificate/" target="_blank">Leadership in Special &amp; Inclusive Education</a></dt>
	<dd>Designed for educational professionals, concerned family members and advocates alike, this online certificate program provides the opportunity to learn and expand your knowledge and skills to support students with learning, social and emotional needs.</dd>
</dl><dl><dt><strong>School of Nursing</strong></dt>
	<dt>
	<ul><li><a href="http://nursing.kumc.edu/academics/certificate-programs/psychiatricmental-health-nurse-practitioner-certificate.html" target="_blank">Psychiatric/Mental Health Nurse Practitioner</a></li>
		<li><a href="http://nursing.kumc.edu/academics/certificate-programs/clinical-research-management.html" target="_blank">Clinical Research Management</a></li>
		<li><a href="http://nursing.kumc.edu/academics/certificate-programs/healthcare-informatics-certificate.html" target="_blank">Healthcare Informatics</a></li>
		<li><a href="http://nursing.kumc.edu/academics/certificate-programs/organizational-leadership.html" target="_blank">Organizational Leadership</a></li>
		<li><a href="http://nursing.kumc.edu/academics/certificate-programs/health-professions-educator-.html" target="_blank">Health Professions Educator</a></li>
	</ul><p> </p>
