        <div class="col-sm-12 col-md-3 scholarships">
            <div class="scholarship-description callout">
                <span class="scholarship-name">Goldwater Scholars</span> receive up to $7,500 annually for undergraduate study in science, technology, engineering, or math.
            </div>
            <div class="scholarship-description callout">
                <span class="scholarship-name">Astronaut Scholarship</span> recipients receive $10,000 to pursue scientific endeavors and help the United States retain its world leadership in technology.
            </div>
            <div class="scholarship-description callout">
                <span class="scholarship-name">Beckman Scholars</span> receive a $21,000 stipend to participate in long-term research in chemistry or molecular biosciences. KU is one of 12 universities in the country chosen to participate.
            </div>
            <div class="scholarship-description callout">
                <span class="scholarship-name">Udall Scholars,</span> chosen for their commitment to studying and protecting the environment, receive up to $5,000 for undergraduate study.
            </div>
            <div class="scholarship-description callout">
                <span class="scholarship-name">Truman Scholars</span> receive up to $30,000 for graduate study and have opportunities to develop leadership skills and prepare for careers in public service.
            </div>
