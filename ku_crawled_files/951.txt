		<h1>Welcome to Remote Support!</h1>
<p>
		You will be asked to download a small Rescue Applet to your computer to begin the session.
</p>
		The Applet will allow you to:<br /><br />
<ul class="padded">
   <li>Chat with the technician(s)</li>
   <li>View a detailed session log</li>
   <li>Allow or deny the technician the right to perform certain actions</li>
   <li>Send individual files to a technician (or to the Lead Technician during a Collaboration Session)</li>
   <li>End Remote Control by clicking the large X at upper left</li>
   <li>End a session by clicking the small X at upper right</li>
</ul>

		Note: The Applet will automatically remove itself from your PC when your session ends.
